Note: you must have a whitelisted URL for your local or test environments to bypass CORS restrictions. These are *.gi.org or *.acggi.net.

Call the stylesheet from our Assets server into your document's <head>.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://assets.gi.org/css/gi.org/style.css">

This is the "full meal deal" in that it contains all of our components, utilities, and helpers in a single, compiled (minified) file.

Optional Components

Call Jquery into your document's <foot>. You can call whatever version (at least 1.11) you desire.

<script src="https://ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/2.1.1/jquery.min.js></script>

If you want basic Bootstrap Javascript component functionality (notably Tabs, Popovers, Tooltips, and Alerts) call these files:

<script src="https://assets.gi.org/js/vendor/tether.min.js></script>
<script src="https://assets.gi.org/vendor/js/bootstrap.min.js></script>

Depending on what you're building, you may eschew Bootstrap or jQuery altogether as your framework of choice may already have much of this functionality or re-factored component libraries (i.e. React Bootstrap) available to you.
