Floats ("Pulls")

.pull-left and .pull-right allows floats on all viewport sizes (similar to .pull-xs-left and .pull-xs-right below)

Responsive Floats

<div class="pull-xs-left">Float left on all viewport sizes</div>
<div class="pull-xs-right">Float right on all viewport sizes</div>
<div class="pull-xs-none">Don't float on all viewport sizes</div>

<div class="pull-sm-left">Float left on viewports sized SM (small) or wider</div>
<div class="pull-md-left">Float left on viewports sized MD (medium) or wider</div>
<div class="pull-lg-left">Float left on viewports sized LG (large) or wider</div>
<div class="pull-xl-left">Float left on viewports sized XL (extra-large) or wider</div>
Tests
Float right on all viewport sizes
Float right on viewports sized LG (large) or wider
Float right on viewports sized XL (extra-large) or wider

Alignments

.text-right and .text-right and .text-center aligns text and inline/inline-block element on all viewport sizes (similar to .text-xs-left/right/center below)

Responsive Alignment

<div class="text-xs-left">Align left on all viewport sizes</div>
<div class="text-xs-right">Align right on all viewport sizes</div>
<div class="text-sm-left">Align left on viewports sized SM (small) or wider</div>
<div class="text-md-left">Align left on viewports sized MD (medium) or wider</div>
<div class="text-lg-left">Align left on viewports sized LG (large) or wider</div>
<div class="text-xl-left">Align left on viewports sized XL (extra-large) or wider</div>
Tests
Align right on all viewport sizes
Align center on viewports sized LG (large) or wider
Align right on viewports sized XL (extra-large) or wider
